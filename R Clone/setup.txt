Rclone with Parallel Transfers (Fastest Tweak)
Rclone khud bohat fast ho sakta hai agar aap default settings ko customize karein. By default, rclone 4 files ek sath upload karta hai. Aap isay barha sakte hain:

Command: rclone copy "C:\folder" mydrive: -P --transfers 10 --checkers 16

Faida: Ye ek waqt mein 10 files upload karega, jis se speed behtar ho jati hai agar aapka internet fast hai.









Ek Choti si Tip:
Agar aapka net bar bar disconnect ho raha ho, toh command ke aakhir mein ye do cheezain add kar dein: --retries 10 --low-level-retries 10

Is se rclone khud hi 10 baar dobara connect karne ki koshish karega, aapko baar baar command nahi likhni paray gi.
